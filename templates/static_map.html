http://www.wheredoyougo.net/{{ map_relative_url }}

is intended for use anywhere on the internet where you might want to display a map of the places you tend to go. Use it as the source of an <img src=""/> HTML tag on your homepage or blog, and watch it update in the background as you check in to new places.

To change the color scheme, zoom level, size, or center location just modify the map above and click to regenerate your static map image.

(Note that I hope to let each user have multiple maps in the future (in case someone spends a lot of time in two different cities, for example), but for now just OAauth into the same Foursquare account with a second Google account.)
